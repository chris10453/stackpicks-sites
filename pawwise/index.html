How We Review Dog Training Programs

There are dozens of "online dog trainers" and most of them are recycled PDFs. We compare each program on practical factors: the training method (we only recommend humane, reward-based, force-free approaches), how the lessons are delivered, whether there's real support, the price, and what cited user feedback reports after a few weeks, when a traceable source is available. Individual outcomes vary, and if a program is thin or outdated, we say so.
