Methodology

How PawWise ranks offers

By the StackPicks research team · contact

StackPicks — an independent comparison publisher. Research and reviews by the StackPicks research team. Contact: stackpicksmedia@gmail.com

We compare the information a reader can inspect before paying: product fit, format, price clarity, rebill clarity, guarantee language, and whether the destination is reachable over HTTPS without an approval or whitelist gate.

Scoring

  • Fit: 30% — matches the stated dog-owner problem.
  • Clarity: 25% — price, format, and terms are easy to find.
  • Practicality: 25% — a reasonable routine or clearly scoped product.
  • Risk: 20% — caveats, recurring terms, and claims are visible.

Source boundaries

We use vendor pages and the ClickBank hoplink response as source material. We do not claim personal use, clinical efficacy, or independent testing. Prices are snapshots, not guarantees. If a page is gated, unavailable, or unclear, we exclude it rather than invent a price.
