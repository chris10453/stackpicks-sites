How we rank

  1. We identify the actual product format from the seller flow.
  2. We record the price visible before payment and state the rebill schedule. If a clean total cannot be verified, we remove the row.
  3. We check whether the page gives a reader enough scope, terms, and risk context to make a more informed click.
  4. We show pros, cons, a score, and one reason for the editorial pick. Scores do not claim efficacy, profitability, or medical suitability.
  5. We cite independent signals only when we can link and characterize them accurately. Otherwise we say there is no independent signal found.

How affiliate money works

Some marked links are ClickBank affiliate links. If a reader purchases after clicking, StackPicks may receive a commission at no extra cost to the reader. That relationship is disclosed beside the commercial path and does not decide the order, score, or top pick. We can earn from a lower-ranked offer; a commission is not proof of quality.

Limits

Prices, shipping, availability, refund terms, and vendor claims can change. We do not enter payment details, buy products, conduct clinical testing, audit trading performance, or treat testimonials as independent evidence. Readers should review the live order terms and consult appropriate professionals.
