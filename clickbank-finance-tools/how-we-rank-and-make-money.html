How we rank finance offers

By the StackPicks research team · contact

StackPicks — an independent finance comparison publisher. Research and reviews by the StackPicks research team.

Three consumer-facing gates

1. Marketplace activity: ClickBank Gravity must be at least 15 at selection time. Gravity is a recent activity signal, not a sales count or quality score.

2. Claims sanity: we must be able to describe the offer without repeating unsupported income, wealth, or guaranteed-results promises.

3. Checkout clarity: the official order flow must show a visible price before payment and state billing and refund terms clearly.

Inventory policy

This is a finance-only inventory. We do not fill the page with supplements, brainwave audio, wellness offers, or unrelated SKUs. Product names appear beside vendor codes so readers can understand what was checked.

If no offer passes, we publish a useful empty state instead of padding the list. Free alternatives are labeled NOT AFFILIATED.

What a score would mean

Our Checkout-Clarity Score describes shopping-information quality only: price visibility, billing clarity, refund clarity, scope clarity, and evidence limits. It is not an efficacy, safety, investment, or outcome score.
