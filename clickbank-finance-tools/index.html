Checkout-Clarity Score rubric

Score name: Checkout-Clarity Score. This is a shopping-information score, not an efficacy, safety, investment-performance, or suitability score.

  • Price visibility (0–2): total shown before payment, including required shipping.
  • Billing clarity (0–2): one-time versus rebills stated in the vendor order flow.
  • Refund clarity (0–2): a definite vendor refund window is visible.
  • Scope clarity (0–2): format, package, and what is included are understandable.
  • Evidence limits (0–2): claims and independent evidence limits are plainly separated.

Tiers: 8.0–10.0 = High clarity · 6.0–7.9 = Moderate clarity · 0–5.9 = Low clarity. “Insufficient info to rate” means a required fact—such as a findable ingredient list—was unavailable; it is not a zero.
