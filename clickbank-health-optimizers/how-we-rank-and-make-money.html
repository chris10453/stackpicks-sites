How we rank and make money

Checkout-Clarity Score rubric

Our Checkout-Clarity Score measures shopping-information quality only: price visibility (0–2), billing clarity (0–2), refund clarity (0–2), scope clarity (0–2), and evidence limits (0–2).

Tiers: 8.0–10.0 High clarity; 6.0–7.9 Moderate clarity; 0–5.9 Low clarity. Supplements without a findable official ingredient list receive insufficient info to rate, not a zero.

Inventory policy

We drop brainwave-audio SKUs and claim-heavy offers when the vendor page would require repeating unsupported claims. We do not backfill unless the replacement passes a claims-sanity read and has a definite pre-payment billing and refund statement. Free alternatives are labeled NOT AFFILIATED.
